Mechanical and Housing

Physical Component Overview

Our housing unit was constructed with african mahogany wood that contains two levels with a total of 4 areas to hold the plants. The housing behind the first level and under the second level houses the water tank and the electrical components.

Dimensions

Overall housing: 22” x 18” x 22”

Platform for plants: 22” x 9” x 1” with .5” depth inwards

Water tank housing: 13” x 11” x 9”

Electrical housing: 8.75” x 9” x 11”

Materials

Mahogany Wood (1” x 12” x 96”)

Carnauba Paste Wax

#16-1/2 x 1 in. Nails

Gorilla Glue (ensure stable holding between main components)
